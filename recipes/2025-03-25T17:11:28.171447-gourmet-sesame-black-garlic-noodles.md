# Gourmet Sesame Black Garlic Noodles

Indulge in the rich, umami flavors of toasted sesame oil and black garlic oil with this exquisite noodle dish. The fusion of these specialty oils creates a symphony of savory notes that elevate this recipe to gourmet status.

## Ingredients:

  * 8 oz (225g) Asian wheat noodles
  * 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  * 1 tbsp black garlic oil
  * 2 cloves black garlic, minced
  * 2 tbsp soy sauce
  * 1 tsp honey
  * 1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  * 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  * 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds



## Instructions:

  1. Boil the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the toasted sesame oil, black garlic oil, minced black garlic, soy sauce, honey, and grated ginger.
  3. Add the cooked noodles to the bowl and toss until well coated with the sauce.
  4. Garnish with sliced green onions and toasted sesame seeds before serving.



### Chef's Tips:

For an extra flavor boost, try adding a splash of black garlic balsamic vinegar to the sauce. It will add a delightful tanginess to the dish.

Feel free to customize the toppings with shredded chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a protein-packed variation.

### Serving Suggestions:

Pair these gourmet noodles with a crisp cucumber salad and a glass of chilled white wine for a luxurious dining experience.
