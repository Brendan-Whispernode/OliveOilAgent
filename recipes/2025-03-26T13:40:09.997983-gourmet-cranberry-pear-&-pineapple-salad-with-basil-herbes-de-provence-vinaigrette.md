# Gourmet Cranberry Pear & Pineapple Salad with Basil-Herbes de Provence Vinaigrette

Indulge in the delightful flavors of this gourmet salad, where the sweetness of cranberry pear white balsamic and pineapple white balsamic meets the aromatic essence of basil-infused olive oil and herbes de Provence olive oil. This recipe is a refreshing and vibrant blend of unique flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

## Ingredients:

  * 6 cups mixed salad greens
  * 1 ripe pear, thinly sliced
  * 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  * 1/2 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  * 1/4 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
  * 1/4 cup crumbled feta or goat cheese
  * 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  * 2 tbsp cranberry pear white balsamic
  * 2 tbsp pineapple white balsamic
  * 1 tbsp basil-infused olive oil
  * 1 tbsp herbes de Provence olive oil
  * Salt and pepper to taste



## Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, sliced pears, dried cranberries, pineapple chunks, and chopped nuts.
  2. In a small jar, combine the extra virgin olive oil, cranberry pear white balsamic, pineapple white balsamic, basil-infused olive oil, herbes de Provence olive oil, salt, and pepper. Close the jar and shake vigorously to emulsify the vinaigrette.
  3. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to coat the ingredients evenly.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled cheese over the top of the salad.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of flavors!



## Chef's Tips:

For a more intense flavor, let the vinaigrette sit for 10-15 minutes before drizzling it over the salad to allow the flavors to meld. You can also add grilled chicken or shrimp to turn this salad into a complete meal.

## Serving Suggestions:

This gourmet salad makes a perfect starter for a dinner party or a delightful light lunch. Pair it with a glass of chilled white wine to complement the fruity and herbal notes of the vinaigrette.
